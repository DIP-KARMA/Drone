Infrastructure Inspection

Solar Farm Thermal Inspection

Client: SunPower Energy

SunPower Energy needed to inspect their 50-hectare solar farm for potential defects and hotspots. Using our Inspecto-X drone equipped with thermal imaging, we conducted a comprehensive inspection that identified 37 defective panels and 12 connection issues. This allowed for targeted maintenance rather than a time-consuming manual inspection of the entire facility.
